About Us

We are dedicated to providing the best services for our clients.

Books: Tyler

Tyler is a sophomore journalism major with a strong passion for sports and storytelling. Outside the classroom, he is a dedicated gym-goer and an avid football fan, interests that fuel his understanding of athletic culture and competition. A loyal Chicago Bears supporter, Tyler closely follows the NFL and enjoys analyzing games, players, and team dynamics. He aspires to become a sports writer after college, combining his academic training with his firsthand appreciation for fitness and athletics to deliver insightful, engaging coverage of the sports world.


Barbells: Tory

Tory is a sophomore computer science major who balances the analytical demands of technology with the discipline of collegiate track and field. Although he only began taking an interest in sports a few years ago, his involvement has grown into a strong passion, culminating in competing at the collegiate level. Alongside his technical studies, Tory enjoys reading philosophy and reflecting on questions of purpose, ethics, and impact. With his computer science degree, he hopes to join a meaningful organization that uses technology to improve everyday life, combining recent athletic discovery, disciplined training, and thoughtful problem-solving to create positive change.


Our History

We’ve known each other since middle school, building a friendship that has grown stronger over the years. Now attending the same college, we continue to support one another through academics, athletics, and everyday life, just as we always have. From shared experiences to new challenges, we push each other to be better while staying grounded in trust and loyalty. More than classmates or teammates, we’re best buddies who have grown together and continue to move forward side by side.
